About Me

Hi! My name is Shree Khambekar, and I am currently a student at the University of Missouri-Kansas City (UMKC). I am a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics & Statistics, both Bachelor of Science. Aside from academics, I enjoy accomplishing things on-campus. Currently, I volunteer as a language partner through UMKC's Applied Language Institute (ALI) for students learning English. I am also a part of UMKC's Professional Career Escalators (PCE) Scholarship Program which builds members' professional skills for future career success. I have also worked with JavaScript, MySQL, React, PHP, and Machine Learning frameworks. I am a recipient of numerous scholarships for tuition assistance, and have made the Dean's List every year in college due to my consistent outstanding academic performance. I am passionate about learning, having a presence, and making an impact in my community. I plan to pursue computer science careers that have an emphasis on math.
